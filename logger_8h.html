enum  logger_verbosity_t {
  OFF , ERROR , WARNING , INFO ,
  DEBUG
}
 Typedef'd enum representing verbosity levels, in increasing order of verbosity from least verbose (ERROR, only logs ERRORs) to most verbose (DEBUG, will log all). When a destination is configured with a certain verbosity, messages logged with that verbosity or LOWER will be sent to the destination. e.g. a destination with verbosity INFO will receive logs logged with verbosity INFO, WARNING and ERROR, but not DEBUG.
